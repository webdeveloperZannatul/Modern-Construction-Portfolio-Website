ABOUT US

Crafting Chennai's Skyline Since 1998

Harsha Constructions has been at the forefront of Tamil Nadu's construction industry for over two decades. Founded with a vision to blend traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation, we have successfully delivered over 250 projects ranging from luxury residences to iconic commercial spaces.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability has earned us the trust of clients across South India. We take pride in our team of experienced architects, engineers, and craftsmen who bring expertise and passion to every project.
